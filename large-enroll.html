Largest Districts Analysis

Our analysis intially led us to understand the public school enrollment trends across the five largest school districts in California. Because the total enrollment varies significantly, we instead plotted the percent changes in enrollment across the state. Through this analysis, we found that four out of the five largest school districts in California had seen decreasing enrollment year-over-year since 2015. Only the Elk Grove Unified District had experienced positive enrollment every year since 2015.
